# 💕 Will You Go On A Date With Me?

A fun, self-contained "ask someone out" web page (inspired by the Instagram reel).

## How to use
Just open `index.html` in any browser — no server or build needed.

## The flow
1. **The big question** — "Will you go on a date with me?" with a **Yes** and a **No** button.
   The **No** button runs away from the cursor (and dodges taps on mobile), so it's
   impossible to press. Every dodge makes the **Yes** button grow and throws out a
   playful taunt.
2. **Plan the date** — after pressing **Yes**, she picks the **day**, the **time**,
   and the **vibe** (coffee, dinner, adventure, movie night, dancing, or "surprise me").
3. **The vibe of the date** — a cute confirmation screen summarizing when, what time,
   and the vibe of the date. 🎉

Includes floating-heart background animation and confetti bursts on each step.
