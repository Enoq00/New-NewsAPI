# 💌 Will You Go On A Date With Me?

A fun, creatively-designed "ask someone out" web page (inspired by the Instagram reel).
Self-contained — just open `index.html` in any browser. No server, no build, no dependencies
(fonts load from Google Fonts when online; it still works offline with system fonts).

## The flow
1. **The question** — "Will you go on a *date* with me?" with a **Yes** and a **No** button.
   The **No** button is impossible to press: it darts away from the cursor, dodges taps on
   mobile, and even flees keyboard focus. Every dodge grows the **Yes** button, swaps the
   mascot's mood, and throws out a playful taunt.
2. **Plan it** — after **Yes**, she picks the **day**, the **time**, and the **vibe**
   (Cozy Coffee, Fancy Dinner, Adventure, Movie Night, Dancing, or Surprise Me).
3. **The vibe of the date** — a "Date Pass" ticket summarizing when / time / vibe, a
   **live countdown** to the date, and a personalized one-liner painting the mood.

## Design touches
- Animated multi-color gradient backdrop with soft glowing orbs
- Glassmorphism cards with a gentle entrance animation
- `Fraunces` display serif + `Quicksand` pairing for a romantic-but-playful tone
- Typewriter intro line, floating hearts, shimmer on the Yes button
- Confetti bursts on every step, a ticket-style payoff, and a live countdown timer
